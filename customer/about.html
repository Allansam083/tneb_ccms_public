About TNEB Complaint Management System

The TNEB Complaint Management System is designed to streamline the process of managing consumer complaints efficiently. Our mission is to ensure transparency, reliability, and prompt resolution of issues faced by consumers.

Key Objectives:

  • Provide a platform for easy complaint registration.
  • Enable real-time tracking of complaints.
  • Enhance communication between consumers and service providers.
  • Reduce response time and improve resolution rates.
